Ingredients:
 - Peanutbutter
 - Jam or Jelly
 - 2 slices of Bread

Directions:
  1. Spread peanutbutter onto one side of the first slice of bread using a knife
  2. Spread jam/jelly onto one side of the second slice of  bread using a knife
  3. Place the first slice of bread on the second so that the peanutbutter and the
     jam/jelly are touching.
